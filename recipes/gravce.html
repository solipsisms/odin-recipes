tavce gravce

description

from wikipedia: "Tavče gravče is a traditional Macedonian dish. It is prepared with fresh beans and can be found in many restaurants in North Macedonia. It is also commonly eaten by the Macedonian diaspora. This meal is baked and served in a traditional unglazed earthenware pot."

ingredients

  • 4 cups northern beans, cleaned
  • 3 tablespoons canola oil
  • 2 large onions
  • 2 dried whole chili peppers
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • chopped parsely
for the roux
  • a half cup of oil
  • 2 tablespoons paprika

directions

  1. place the beans in a 5-quart pot and cover with water about 2 inches above the beans
  2. pour in the oil.
  3. add in quartered onions and dried chili peppers
  4. cook the beans over medium-high heat until soft, about 1.5 to 2 hours
  5. stir often and add warm water as it evaporates. you are looking for a stw-like consistency
  6. onces the beans are cooked, do not remove from heat and start to prepare the roux
  7. slightly heat the oil in a fryin pan over medium heat
  8. stir in the paprika and keep stirring until just thoroughly combined and fragrant
  9. pour the roux into the pot with the beans and stir
  10. mix in a tablespoon of salt and continue to boil for 2 to 3 minutes
  11. remove from heat
  12. preheat oven to 400
  13. carefully pour the beans into a large casserole dish, add enough water to just cover the beans
  14. place the sliced rounds of onions and the cooked chili peppers on top as garnish
  15. add salt and pepper to taste
  16. bake the beans for 30 minutes, or until a crusty layer forms on top of the beans
  17. remove and let cool 15 minutes before serving
  18. add chopped parsley for garnish
